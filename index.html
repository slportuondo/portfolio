About Me

As a Richmond native, Ohio State graduate, brief Chicago resident, and now Angeleno, Sebastian Portuondo is quite comfortable adjusting to change.
Sebastian has been tinkering with software since early high school. Initially focused on animation and design, he learned the intricacies of software platforms like PhotoShop, Blender, and AutoCAD. He later made a shift to music, and learned to engineer professional-quality music in less than a year by leveraging similarities he found between his Digital Audio Workstations and his past digital design experiences. In college, Sebastian studied Strategic Communication, and honed his analysis and pattern recognition skills through deep research into human communication and interaction patterns.
Throughout his education journey, Sebastian has taken classes (both structured and self-directed) in full-stack MERN and LAMP web development, software engineering principles in Java, and UX design.
Sebastian is deeply interested in dissecting the problems that humans face, while navigating the bottlenecks that make those problems difficult to solve. Regardless of industry, he is sure to challenge outdated solutions to modern problems.
